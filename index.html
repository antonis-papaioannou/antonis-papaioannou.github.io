About

Hi, my name is Antonis Papaioannou and I am currently Postdoctoral researcher at the Information Systems Laboratory at the Institute of Computer Science (ICS) of Foundation for Research and Technology - Hellas (FORTH).

I received my PhD from Computer Science Department of University of Crete, Greece, under the supervision of professor Kostas Magoutis. My PhD dissertation focuses on efficient adaptation mechanisms for improving performance during internal or external changes in distributed data stores
